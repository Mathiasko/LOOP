Welcome to LOOP

LOOP Fitness is a unique circular workout concept that ensures you a well-balanced whole body unique combination of exercises that is performed on 16 fitness machines set up in a circle. The 16 carefully selected machines together provide an effective training of both muscle strength and fitness.

LOOP Fitness is for everyone

People of all ages, sizes and shapes can benefit from training in LOOP. There is room for everyone. We offer a nice environment and among other things, a nice cup of coffee or tea after exercise.

Get a head start!

Get a head start!

If you start building healthy habits now, you’ll be able to avoid unhealthy choices during the holidays. Waiting until January 1st will only delay the challenges that you will need to face later. So start now, and put an end to the procrastination and stress that comes with it.

Did you Know?

Exercising has been show to improve mood, decrease feelings of depression, anxiety and stress. In addition it increases the production of endorphins, which are known to help produce positive feelings.

CONSISTENCY IS THE KEY TO PROGRESS

You won’t fulfill your fitness goals with a January resolution that’s abandoned by February. At LOOP Fitness, we encourage consistency by providing you with enjoyable and effective training, but it’s up to you to stay dedicated to your fitness goals.
